How it works.

  • Step 1

    Create an account using the Android or iOS app on your phone, or even use the web version. All work exactly the same and data is synced automatically.

  • Step 2

    Verify your account by clicking on the link in the email that was sent when you created your account. After logging in complete your profile, and then you can start adding or inviting family members.

  • Step 3

    Add family members directly or send them an invitation to join your family. Once they accept by clicking on the link in the invatation email, they will be automatically added to your family.

  • Step 4

    Answer the questions in My History to generate a set of data to work with, and then start sharing stories and memories experienced by you and your family. That's it!
